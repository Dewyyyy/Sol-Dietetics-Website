What We're Looking For

The kind of dietitian
who fits our model.

We partner with RDNs who want to spend more time on meaningful client work and less time on admin, marketing, and logistics — because we handle all of that.

Registered Dietitian Nutritionist (RDN)

Our core role. You'll deliver 1-on-1 virtual consultations, design personalized meal plans, and guide clients through their nutrition journey — all from your own schedule.

Virtual-First Practice

Everything is remote. No clinic, no commute. You work with clients through our secure HIPAA-compliant platform on a schedule that fits your life.

Fitness & Wellness Enthusiast

Our client base comes from gyms, CrossFit affiliates, and wellness studios. A genuine interest in performance nutrition, weight management, or active lifestyles is a strong fit.

Growth-Oriented Mindset

We're early-stage and expanding fast. We're looking for dietitians who are excited to help shape what virtual nutrition care looks like at scale — not just fill a slot.

Qualifications

What we require.

  • Active RDN (Registered Dietitian Nutritionist) credential in good standing
  • State licensure where required for telehealth practice
  • Comfortable with virtual client sessions (video, messaging)
  • Strong written communication for remote client plans and messaging
  • Reliable internet connection and a quiet, professional workspace
  • Experience with or genuine interest in fitness, sports, or weight-management nutrition
