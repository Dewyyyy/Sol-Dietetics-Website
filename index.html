The Process

Simple to start,
powerful in practice.

We handle all aspects of the dietetic experience — from initial client consultations to personalized meal planning and grocery delivery — so you can focus on what you do best.

01

We establish a simple revenue share agreement tailored to your client base and business model. No overhead, no risk.

02

You refer clients who want nutrition coaching. We take it from there — intake, assessment, and ongoing guidance.

03

Our registered dietitians deliver expert, personalized nutrition plans and 1-on-1 coaching — fully virtual, fully managed by us.

04

Clients receive curated, dietitian-approved grocery deliveries matched to their nutrition plan — a premium add-on they'll love.

05

You earn a percentage of every client's spend. Transparent reporting, consistent payouts, zero administrative burden.
