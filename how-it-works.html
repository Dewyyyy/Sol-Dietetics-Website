Client Journey

What your members experience.

The nutrition program is your gym's offering — your branding, your pricing, your client relationships. Sol stays behind the curtain delivering the clinical service.

Phase 1

Enrollment & Intake

Members enroll through your gym's nutrition program signup. They complete a comprehensive health history, goals survey, and upload recent labs if available. The intake is clinical-grade — not a generic quiz.

Phase 2

Initial Consult

A 45-minute virtual session with a California-licensed RD. They review the intake, set a baseline, and build a personalized protocol tied to the member's training and goals.

Phase 3

Program Delivery

Members receive their custom meal plan, macro targets, and protocol directly inside the Healthie portal. Adherence and progress are tracked in real time — and the plan adjusts as the member evolves.

Phase 4

Weekly Check-Ins

Structured weekly reviews via the Healthie portal. The RD reviews adherence, adjusts targets, answers questions, and keeps members accountable between sessions.

Phase 5

Ongoing Optimization

As the member progresses, the RD layers in advanced protocols, supplement recommendations (via Fullscript), and training-aligned nutrition strategies.

The Compliance Layer

Built right the first time.

Nutrition is a regulated field. Every Sol Dietetics program is designed to meet state licensure law, OOP rules, and HIPAA from day one — so you never carry the clinical liability.

Licensure

California-Licensed RD

All programs are prescribed and supervised by a Registered Dietitian licensed in California. We only operate in states where our RD holds active licensure.

Oversight

Physician Oversight

A supervising physician reviews clinical protocols and co-signs medically sensitive cases. This is what separates Sol from a coaching app — it's real healthcare.

Insurance

100% OOP Compliant

The program is delivered entirely on an out-of-pocket basis. No insurance billing, no CPT codes, no Medicare exposure — which means no anti-kickback risk for gyms referring members.

Data

HIPAA via Healthie

All Protected Health Information lives in Healthie's HIPAA-compliant EMR. Gyms never see clinical data; the RD owns the clinical relationship with the member.
